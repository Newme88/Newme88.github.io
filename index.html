Five pieces of innocence

This photo was taken by me in XiHu. When I was wandering around the XiHu，I found people surrounding around a tree，feeding the squirrel. I was surprised that the squirrel was not afraid of people. So I took a picture. Actually，the original protagonist was the squirrel. But，just now when I reviewed it ，I found the father and daughter was the more attractive scenery. I swore I didn’t notice them when I took the photo. Look their facial expression. How happy they were！
I named it as “Five pieces of Innocence”，describing the father ,daughter ，the squirrel，the man who held the corn and me！Haha～ We were all the people with innocence.

We need to be a life lover by discovering the meaningful moment,don’t we?

Swimming makes me happy!

2021.5.1

It’s been 8 years since I last swam. I had so many classes in my junior and senior high school,having no time to swim. But I really love it. I still remember that I was taught by a colleague of my father when my class was over in my primary school period. When we got there ,there always were seldom people cause it’s late and the temperature was low. But I didn’t care about it，it couldn’t stifle the fire in me. I was excited instead！I wouldn’t feel awkward or shameful as a beginner because of less people. In the process of learning to swim,I drank a lot water of the poll. Oh，it tasted terrible，the smell of disinfectant fluid ,I guess. I learned carefully step by step. I could feel the progress . A few weeks later,I overcame the fear on water and could swim on my own. Haha～.I got it. I was so proud of it.
This holiday,the Labor Day,I found a swimming poll to swim. I was afraid that I lost this capability. Guess what?I caught that feeling naturally when I jumped into water. So happy for me to come back to the water. I swam like a fish,touching the softness of the water. At first ,i couldn’t swim to the other side without breaking. Several times later, I changed the breathing pattern from thoracic breathing to the abdominal breathing. And I could swim for a long distance with less energy. How important the breathing pattern is！
I want to swim in the deep area with 2m depth next time.
